Experience

The travel time on the river takes approximately 30 minutes during summer (Faster flow rate) and 40 to 50 minutes the rest of the year. The length is around 7.5 kilometers. During the summer, the river is recommended only to people with experience.

Cost

The price for rafting varies between $ 30 and $45. It all depends on the size of the group and the date you choose for the activity. Bigger groups during weekdays are cheaper.

Tips

Assume that you will be wet and depending on the weather you must take into account the following:
1. Repellent and sunscreen.
2. Polo shirt, preferably synthetic material.
3. Shorts or light undergarments.
4. Water sandals or any footwear that can get wet, preferably one that holds well to your foot.
5. Hat, sunglasses
6. Bring another change of clothes for the end of the tour.
7. Photos: Don't worry about taking photos during the trip, we will take care of that. It is included as part of the experience.
