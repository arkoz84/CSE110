Movies

The movies can be group into 3 depending on the cast: The Original Series (1971-1991), The Next Generation (1994-2002) and the Kelvin Timeline (2009-2016). In total there are currently 13 movies.

TV Series

The Star Trek franchise has had a total of 13 television series, spanning over 47 seasons and 905 episodes. It started on 1969 and has 3 currently running shows.
